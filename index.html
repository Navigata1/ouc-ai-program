The Moment We're In

In 1975, schools banned calculators.
They were wrong.

Every generation faces a tool so powerful it scares us. The printing press. The calculator. The internet. Each time, the institutions that embraced it early didn't just survive — they led.

AI is that tool for our generation. The gap between those who understand it and those who don't is growing every single day. This isn't about Silicon Valley. It's about making sure our community — our youth, our entrepreneurs, our elders, our church — isn't left behind while the world rewrites itself.

Our Approach

Sharpen the Mind.
